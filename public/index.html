Welcome to STAP school ERP

Stap is a unique school management platform,that manages all interactions of the different stakeholders i.e students,teachers,administrators and parents. Stap is said to be unique because it manages all core school functions,saves administrative time, increases school-to-parent interaction and opens new channels for learning through technology for students. With stap parents can easily access student's result online, students can access e-learning education, admins can improve and manage all the systems or mechanisms of the school .
