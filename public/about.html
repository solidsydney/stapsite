An Introduction of stap

STAP which stands for students,teachers,administrators and parents is a unique school management platform,that manages all interactions of the different stakeholders i.e students,teachers,administrators and parents. Stap is said to be unique because it manages all core school functions,saves administrative time, increases school-to-parent interaction and opens new channels for learning through technology for students. With stap parents can easily access student's result online, students can access e-learning education, admins can improve and manage all the systems or mechanisms of the school. Here are some modules of stap:

Student Data System: Gives access to student's bio-data and transcripts. It also serves as a backup and protects students' records from fire.

Internal Messaging System: With this you will be able to send notifications to parents and employees in real time via SMS. With this system you don't have to buy sms units from bulk sms providers. SMS sent with the internal messaging system is free within defined limits.

E-Learning System: This helps you to have online line classes as well as give learners the opportunity of accessing study materials from homes.

Online Result System: With this teachers can easily manage examinations and parent will also be able to access their children's result from anywhere and at anytime.

Finance Management: With this you can manage all financial dealings ranging from school fees collection and monitoring to expenses, etc.

Human Resource Management: This helps in managing employees both teaching and non-teaching staff. It gives you the ability to contact them at anytime as well as view their profiles and manage their salaries.
